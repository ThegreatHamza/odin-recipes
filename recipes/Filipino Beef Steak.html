Filipino Beef Steak

Description

This beef steak is a very easy recipe to make. A tender cut of beef is sliced thin, then marinated with lemon juice and soy sauce for at least an hou

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds New York strip steak, sliced thin
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Â¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Remove beef slices from marinade, shaking to remove any excess liquid. Discard marinade.
  3. Working in batches, fry beef slices in hot oil until they start to firm and are reddish-pink and juicy in the center, 2 to 4 minutes per side. Transfer beef slices to a serving platter.
  4. Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion and garlic in hot oil until onion is golden brown, 5 to 7 minutes; spoon over beef slices
